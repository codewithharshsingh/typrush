How It Works

  • Choose Your Mode: Choose Your Mode: Time (15s, 30s, 60s, 120s), Words (10, 25, 50), or Custom (set your own time or word count).
  • Start Typing: The text appears on screen. Start typing in the input area — errors are highlighted instantly.
  • View Results: When the challenge ends, see your WPM, Accuracy, and Progress right away.
  • Restart & Practice Again: Hit restart to load fresh text and try again.
